This is INFORMAQ

The Information Network for Fair and Open Real-Time Monitoring of Air Quality.

INFORMAQ is a distributed network of air quality sensors that measures air quality and locates major sources of air pollutants. It creates actionable information on harmful airborne particulates.

The Big Picture .

Airborne particulates cause a myriad of diseases.

Personal health and environment monitoring is increasingly becoming popular .

Airbone particulates are the cause of many lawsuits including Indianhead Estates, CO .

Vision/Goals for the Project

Affordable to the household consumer .

Generate trusted data .

Cooperative network of sensors .

The go-to tool for enforcing air quality regulations.

Target Groups .

Personal / Private - homeowners concerned with air quality impact for nearby industry .

Commercial / Companies - clients concerned with complying with regulations & perceptions .

Regulatory Agencies - clients concerned validating compliance .
